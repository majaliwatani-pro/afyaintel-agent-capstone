# AfyaIntel Vision 2030

AfyaIntel begins as a Kaggle AI Agents capstone, but the long-term vision is larger: a responsible African health-intelligence layer that strengthens primary-care operations without replacing clinicians or national health systems.

## North Star

Build localized, offline-capable, multilingual, and interoperable health intelligence for facilities that cannot depend on continuous connectivity or large technical teams.

## Three horizons

1. **Prototype credibility:** local deterministic tools, bilingual reporting, safety routing, evaluation, and portfolio evidence.
2. **Pilot credibility:** supervised facility demonstration, clinician feedback, de-identified data, FHIR validation, and governance approval.
3. **Institutional credibility:** partnerships with health authorities, universities, facility networks, and responsible funders.

## Principle

AfyaIntel should amplify frontline health workers. It must never replace clinical judgment, professional accountability, or public-sector health governance.
