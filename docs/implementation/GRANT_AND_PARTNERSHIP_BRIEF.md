# AfyaIntel Grant and Partnership Brief

## One-sentence vision

AfyaIntel helps low-resource Tanzanian health facilities turn local-language symptom and medicine-stock signals into safe, offline-first operational intelligence under human clinical governance.

## Why now

Frontline facilities face paper-based records, intermittent connectivity, delayed stock reporting, fragmented health information systems, and limited Swahili/Swanglish clinical NLP support.

## Innovation

AfyaIntel combines:

1. Offline-first facility workflow support.
2. Bilingual English/Swahili operational assistance.
3. Deterministic stock, expiry, forecast, and sync tools.
4. Human-reviewed clinical NLP cue extraction.
5. FHIR-compatible interoperability planning.
6. Privacy-preserving audit metadata and approval controls.

## What support is needed

- Clinical review partners
- De-identified historical stock data
- Facility workflow observation
- Swahili/Swanglish terminology review
- Pilot devices and hosting support
- Data protection and ethics review guidance

## Expected impact

- Earlier identification of stock pressure
- Improved facility reporting discipline
- Better local-language digital-health support
- Stronger bridge between facility workflow, district dashboards, and national systems
