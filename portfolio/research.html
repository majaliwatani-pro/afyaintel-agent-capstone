Research Foundation

From Kaggle prototype to responsible health-informatics innovation.

AfyaIntel Hub DSRM foundation

The broader research frames AfyaIntel as an offline-first generative health intelligence, clinical NLP, predictive supply-chain, and interoperability framework for Tanzanian low-resource primary healthcare settings.

Prototype slice implemented

  • Local medicine-stock intelligence and shortage detection.
  • Swahili/English operational reporting.
  • Human-reviewed clinical NLP cue extraction.
  • Transparent stock-out risk baseline.
  • FHIR-compatible inventory bundle export.
  • Privacy-preserving audit and sync queue demonstration.

Clinical boundary

AfyaIntel is decision support only. It does not replace licensed clinicians, clinical judgment, ethical approval, regulatory authorization, or national EMR systems.
